Account Deletion

If you want to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to the app using your account credentials.
  2. Go to the Settings > Account section.
  3. Click on the "Delete Account" button and confirm your choice.

If you encounter any issues, you can contact our support team:

plantcycle10@gmail.com

By deleting your account, all your personal data will be permanently removed, except for data we are required to retain by law.
